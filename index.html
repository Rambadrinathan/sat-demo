How It Works

Your Google Sheets stay as they are. The agent reads and writes them.

1
Upload your season data to Google Drive
Booking chart, availability grid, activity chart, transport bookings, rate cards — exactly as you use them today.
2
Agent learns your property
Rooms, rates (published + 430 agent-specific contracted rates), activities, transport fleet, invoice templates, GST rules. Builds a compounding knowledge base.
3
Connect Telegram (or WhatsApp)
The reservations manager messages the bot. Natural language — "Check availability Feb 10-14" or "What does Bridging Journeys owe us?"
4
Agent queries live sheets, responds in seconds
Reads the actual Google Sheet, calculates GST, cross-references payments, searches Drive for documents. Every number is real.
5
Knowledge compounds every season
An LLM Wiki synthesizes patterns — occupancy trends, agent performance, refund rates, pricing insights. Gets smarter each year.
